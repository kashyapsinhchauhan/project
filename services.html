SERVICES

1..Book borrowing: The primary service, allowing members to borrow books, magazines, DVDs, and other materials.
2..Reference assistance: Librarians help patrons find information and conduct research.
3..Computer and internet access: Many libraries provide free computer and internet access to the public.
4.. Programs and events: Storytime for kids, book clubs, author readings, and workshops are common.
5.. Digital resources: Access to e-books, audiobooks, online databases, and academic journals.
6..Interlibrary loan: Borrowing materials from other libraries that the local library doesn't have.
7..Study spaces: Quiet areas for studying or group study rooms.
